AcroTac: A Finger-shaped Optical Tactile Sensor with Sensing Surface Extending to the Hyponychium for Dexterous Manipulation

Abstract

AcroTac is a novel finger-shaped optical tactile sensor designed for dexterous manipulation. Unlike traditional optical tactile sensors with limited sensing areas, AcroTac features a sensing surface that extends to the hyponychium (fingertip) region. This design allows for more precise feedback during complex manipulation tasks and contact at the very edge of the fingertip. (Please replace this text with your specific abstract content).
