AcroTac demonstrates high-resolution tactile feedback and robust grasping capabilities in dexterous manipulation tasks.

Abstract

AcroTac is a novel finger-shaped optical tactile sensor specifically engineered to address the challenges of dexterous robotic manipulation. By extending the sensing surface to the hyponychium area, it provides the robot with unprecedented tactile awareness at the very tip of the finger. This design allows for more precise control and feedback during complex interactions with diverse objects...
