AcroTac: A Finger-shaped Optical Tactile Sensor with Sensing Surface Extending to the Hyponychium for Dexterous Manipulation

Demonstration of AcroTac's high-resolution tactile sensing and its application in dexterous manipulation tasks.

Abstract

AcroTac is a novel finger-shaped optical tactile sensor designed for dexterous manipulation. Unlike traditional sensors, its sensing surface extends to the hyponychium (fingertip area), enabling delicate interaction and robust feedback during complex grasping tasks.
